- Nestled along the Sword Coast, Baldur's Gate is a city of duality—where opulence and squalor coexist in unsettling harmony. A hub for traders, travelers, and adventurers alike, its cobbled streets buzz with commerce, its taverns echo with tales, and its dark alleys whisper secrets best left untold.
- But underneath the city's façade lies a labyrinth of political machinations, illicit activities, and shadow organizations. Governed by the Council of Four and kept in check by the enigmatic Flaming Fist mercenary company, Baldur's Gate is ever teetering on the edge of prosperity and chaos. It's a place where fortunes are made, alliances are broken, and where ancient prophecies ominously speak of cataclysmic events yet to unfold.
