- ### Background
	- Toad hails from the Grung village of [[Chult/Dungrunglung]], a realm forever marred by the tragic event known as the [[Massacre of Dungrunglung]], where they narrowly escaped death by bullywugs. In the absence of his 108 fallen elder brothers, Toad inherited the family ship which has been contracted out to [[Sneev]], a past business associate. A member of the red Grung caste—scholars and magic users by tradition—Toad is now at a precarious juncture, seeking both respect within his caste and safety for his tribe.
- ### Early Life and Values
	- Born into a caste system that predetermined his life's path, Toad was assigned to the red Grung caste when his colors came in. Growing up, he was often overshadowed by his more powerful sorcerer brothers who considered him weak and ineffectual. Toad didn't share the tribe's practice of keeping slaves, which only heightened the elders' scorn for him. Losing 108 brothers in a feud with the bullywugs devastated the tribe and marked a critical turning point in Toad's life, compelling him to take action.
- ### Personality Traits
	- Toad can be fiercely territorial, but values fairness and mutual respect among his crew. He enjoys the thrill of new ports and the camaraderie over a flagon of ale. Though prone to stretching the truth for a good tale, his friends know he's reliable when it counts. However, he cannot resist pocketing loose trinkets, and feels superior to any creature he can command.
- ### Appearance
	- Toad stands at 3.5 feet, with warty, poisonous red skin that signifies his caste. He has large black eyes that miss little, yet reveal less. Weighing just 30 pounds, he is bald and asexual, as is common for his species.
- ### Profession and Skills
	- Sailor by trade and a red Grung by caste, Toad is competent in both seafaring and elemental magics.
- ### Goals and Beliefs
	- Toad's primary goals involve reclaiming his and his tribe's lost honor by driving off the bullywugs and finding a new sanctuary. He believes that stories about cursed islands are just tales created to ward off seekers of powerful objects. Toad is tied to the island by rumors of a powerful amulet with powers over death itself. It is believed to be entombed in a shipwreck by the shores.

	- When in doubt, sneak about.

- ### Bonds
	- Toad’s deepest bond is to his inherited ship, which he will protect at all costs—even over the lives of his crew. While his relationship with [[Sneev]] is professional, it is clear that Toad will go to great lengths to ensure the welfare of his tribe and ship.
