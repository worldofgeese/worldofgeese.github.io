- Overlooking the shimmering Chionthar River, Bloomridge stands as a beacon of prosperity and goodwill in Baldur's Gate. Known for its refined gardens and stately homes, the district has gained fame for its community of scholars and philanthropists. The benevolent temples of Bloomridge further elevate its reputation, drawing crowds for their celebrated yearly potlucks and charitable acts that bring joy and comfort to the city's inhabitants.
