- ### Background
	- Born amidst the sizzling fires and molten rivers of the Elemental Plane of Fire, Ember is the offspring of an unusual union between an efreeti and a fanatical human worshiper of [[Kossuth]]. Her mother, a woman of unstable mind and dubious ethics, took part in a bizarre sex ritual. Ember was the result.
- ### Early Life and Values
	- Ember was primarily raised by her human mother, who instilled in her a twisted set of morals deeply influenced by the tyrannical efreeti culture and the fire-worshiping doctrines of Kossuth. However, a twist of fate saw her kidnapped and returned to the Material Plane. There Ember found her way into the den of brass dragons who taught her Draconic.
- ### Personality Traits
	- Though she strives to do good, Ember is driven more by her personal interests than any altruistic motives. Her initial exposure to skewed ethics and questionable morals makes her a bit of a conundrum; she's a gambler, spendthrift, and an adept liar but possesses a good heart. Although she tries to use flattery to get her way, she's not particularly good at it. She acts much like a Robin Hood figure, and her trickster nature is a byproduct of her upbringing—[[Ember's Mentor]] taught her to be the way she is. While courageous in some respects, Ember can be quite the coward when the stakes are too high.
- ### Appearance
	- Her stunning beauty is both mesmerizing and disconcerting, featuring vibrant orange, flaming hair and striking amber eyes. The air around her almost shimmers with heat, and her gaze can be as piercing as it is unsettling.
- ### Profession and Skills
	- Ember found a home with a roving circus troupe in the Material Plane, a haven for misfits like her. Here, she learned to wield a bow and arrow, combining her innate fire abilities for enthralling performances. She also dabbles in peddling fake potions and elixirs, making use of her charlatan background which provided her with an array of colorful, stoppered bottles.
- ### Goals and Beliefs
	- Her stage name, "Ember," embodies her essence and mission. She passionately worships Kossuth and believes in the concept of purification by fire. She's on a personal quest to hunt down a great dragon, to be bathed in its flame: the ultimate purification. A fellow ranger named Sneev knows the location of [[Stormwreck Isle]]—mentioned in a tome she discovered—that could hold the key to locating her draconic adversary.
- ### Instincts
	- When truth's askew, add a twist or two.
	- When dice are in sight, let luck take flight.
- ### Bonds
	- **[[Ember's Mentor]]:** Ember was taken under the wing of a diminutive dwarven man who fancied himself the "smallest man in the world." As the de facto ringleader of a troupe of circus sideshow performers, he saw Ember's unusual nature as a ticket to draw more crowds and make more money. Under his tutelage, Ember honed her skills as a charlatan, learning the art of deception, flattery, and spectacle. His influence is a double-edged sword, shaping her into the person she is but also reinforcing some of her less savory qualities.
