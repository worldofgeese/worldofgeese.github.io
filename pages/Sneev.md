- Sneev is no ordinary kobold; he's a self-proclaimed knight with grand ambitions, ever in service to his mighty dragon god, [[Bahamut]]. With a miniature sword at his side and a code of honor written on scraps of parchment, he considers himself a chivalrous protector of the weak and a loyal friend to his allies. According to the inscrutable commands of his dragon master, Sneev has been dispatched to lead the [[Covenant of Silver]]'s mission to [[Stormwreck Isle]], awaiting further mysterious instructions.
- Recent surge in dragon activity suggests that Sneev may have embarked on secret missions for his enigmatic master. Most notably, whispers abound that some of the dragon's offspring have been stolen—could Sneev be on a clandestine quest to retrieve them?
