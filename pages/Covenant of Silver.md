- Once a year, members of the [[Baldur's Gate/Bloomridge/Temple of Bahamut]], [[Baldur's Gate/Bloomridge/Temple of Selûne]], and the [[Baldur's Gate/Bloomridge/Temple of Solonor Thelandira]] donate faithful warriors on a mission to light the darkest places of the Sword Coast. This is the Covenant of Silver, named for the silver scales of [[Bahamut]], the silver arrows of [[Solonor Thelandira]], and the silver tears of [[Selûne]].
