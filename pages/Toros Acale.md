- ### Background
	- A shipworker and folk hero, Toros dreamed to become a hero like the adventurers who rescued survivors of the [[Eruption of Mount Hotenow]]. Toros hails from [[Neverwinter Wood/Thundertree]].
	- Last year, the yearly [[Covenant of Silver]] mission sent from [[Baldur's Gate/Bloomridge]] sailed to the [[Neverwinter/Docks District]] seeking repairs from an attack of Sahuagin dedicated to [[Umberlee]] . Toros overheard the mission's leader, [[Sneev]], speak of a threat to [[Stormwreck Isle]] , under the protection of his long-lost mentor, [[Runara]].
- ### Early Life and Values
	- Born 129 years ago in [[Neverwinter Wood/Thundertree]], Toros lived through the eruption of Mount Hotenow when he was just ninety-nine, young for an elf. The traumatic event shaped him, setting him on the hero's path. He worships [[Solonor Thelandira]], the third deity of the [[Covenant of Silver]].
- ### Personality Traits
	- Toros is driven by an unquenchable thirst for heroism. He's confident, perhaps overly so, in his own abilities and seeks to inspire others. When he sets his mind on a goal, he'll follow through, heedless of whatever obstacles may come in his path. However, this strong focus sometimes blinds him to evident dangers and his own shortcomings.
- ### Appearance
	- Standing at 5'10", with a medium build weighing 131lb, Toros has fair skin, brown hair, and green eyes. His physique tells stories of years working on ships.
- ### Beliefs
	- I will always protect the weak and the victimized unless it stands in the way of becoming a hero. Heroism is the heroism of fairytales.
- ### Instincts
	- If I see a bully, do violence.
- ### Bonds
	- [[Runara]] first awoke Toros to the path of the hero and mentored Toros until her sudden disappearance years ago. The arrival of the [[Covenant of Silver]] to [[Neverwinter]] and news of Runara alive on [[Stormwreck Isle]] calls Toros to fulfill his destiny.
