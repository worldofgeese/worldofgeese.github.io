- ### Background
	- Ashley, originally an Astral Elf known by a name only her unknown parents are aware of, is a 26-year-old half-elf acolyte who resides in [[Baldur's Gate/Bloomridge]]. Orphaned and lost in the Prime Material Plane, she was taken in by the local [[Baldur's Gate/Bloomridge/Temple of Selûne]], which renamed her and raised her within its faith. She adheres strictly to her alignment of Lawful Good and is heavily involved in the religious community of Baldur’s Gate.
- ### Early Life and Values
	- Hailing from the [[Baldur's Gate/Bloomridge]] area, Ashley grew up without her birth parents and was instead raised in the Temple of Selûne. The temple assigns new names to each orphan, further cementing her connection to her faith and detaching her from her unknown origins. From a young age, she was steeped in the teachings of Selûne and came to see the goddess's ways as the epitome of goodness and law.
- ### Personality Traits
	- Ashley is quite sheltered, having spent the majority of her life within the temple's walls. As such, she finds it challenging to relate to people outside her religious community. She enjoys the finer things in life, including good food and high society, which she's experienced among the temple's elite. Her primary goal is to prove her worthiness to Selûne, making her uncomfortable around concepts and beings linked to Shar or darkness. She is inflexible in her thinking, a flaw that arises from her sheltered upbringing and rigid adherence to her faith's teachings.
- ### Appearance
	- Ashley has unique eyes where the pupils take on the shape of moons and gleam with an inner light. Her appearance otherwise is yet to be defined, aligning with her still-evolving understanding of her self and her origins.
- ### Profession and Skills
	- As an acolyte, Ashley is versed in religious texts, rites, and rituals associated with Selûne. Her devotion grants her the respect of fellow worshipers and the ability to carry out Selûne's ceremonies. She also has some command over magic related to the Twilight domain.
- ### Goals and Beliefs
	- Ashley holds some radical beliefs, including a conviction that women are inherently superior to men and are the chosen of [[Selûne]]. She seeks to convert enough people to gain divine notice, aspiring to be elevated to a celestial plane. To this end, she prioritizes the advancement of women's interests above those of men, believing that only women can serve Selûne as the goddess wishes.
- ### Instincts
	- Ashley is driven to protect the temple and its faith, showing a willingness to act decisively when either is threatened. She is also instinctually wary of any energies or entities linked to Shar or other forms of darkness.
- ### Bonds
	- She has been given a mission by her sister superiors to redeem a cleric named [[Varnoth]], who has gone rogue on [[Stormwreck Isle]]. This mission intertwines with her protective instinct towards her temple, adding urgency and a sense of destiny to her quest. She would also do anything to protect the temple where she was raised and serves.
	- Ashley is a young, devout individual wrestling with a dual identity—both as an orphan of unknown origins and as a chosen servant of Selûne. Her sheltered existence and the inflexibility of her beliefs make her both an earnest and a challenging person. Whether she will achieve her aspirations of divine elevation through rigid orthodoxy or come to see the nuance in her black-and-white world remains to be seen.
